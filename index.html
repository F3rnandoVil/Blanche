Our Story

Founded in 2010, Blanche began as a small neighborhood bakery with a passion for quality ingredients and traditional recipes. Over the years, we've evolved to focus on what we do best: artisanal cookies featuring premium chocolate and nuts.

Each cookie is handcrafted daily in our bakery using locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible. We believe that simple, high-quality ingredients create the most delicious treats.
